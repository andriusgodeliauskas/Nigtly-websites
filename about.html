The Origin

It began in the misty hills of Ecuador. We don't just buy beans; we adopt trees. Every bar of Velvet Cocoa is a testament to the soil, the rain, and the patience of our farmers.

Our "Bean to Bar" philosophy means zero shortcuts. We roast at low temperatures to preserve the delicate floral notes that mass production destroys.

"Chocolate is not a sweet. It is an experience of time and place."

Today, our atelier in Vilnius combines traditional stone-grinding techniques with modern avant-garde recipes. Gold flakes, chili infusion, sea salt from the Dead Sea—we experiment so you can experience.
